题目

Problem

小乔把数字方阵按图折两次，再在箭头黑点处打孔。还会打到哪些数？

Joanna folds the number square twice as shown, then punches the black spot. Which numbers are also punched?

实验：折两次 → 自己点格打孔 → 展开看洞

Lab: fold twice → tap a cell to punch → unfold

对照题目图，折好后自己点一个格子打孔（可换点）。展开后看哪些数有洞，再选选项。不会自动告诉你该点哪。

Match the figure: after folding, tap any cell to punch (you can change). Unfold to see which numbers get holes — nothing is pre-punched for you.

对照展开后的洞，选选项
