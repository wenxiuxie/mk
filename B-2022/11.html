明信片推理

Postcard Logic

2022 等级 B · 第 11 题 · 4 分
2022 Level B · Q11 · 4 points

题目

Problem

五张明信片寄给五位朋友。根据线索判断：小明收到哪一张？

Five postcards to five friends. From the clues: which card did Mike get?

实验：点线索排除 → 在表格里打 ✓ / ✕

Lab: tap clues to filter → mark ✓ / ✕ in the grid

① 点一条线索，符合的卡会变绿高亮；再点卡片可打叉排除。
② 在下方表格里：点格子在「空 / ✓ / ✕」之间切换，把人和卡对应起来。
③ 最后点选：小明是哪张？

① Tap a clue — matching cards turn green; tap a card to cross it out.
② In the grid, cycle empty / ✓ / ✕ to match people and cards.
③ Finally choose: which card is Mike’s?

提示：先确定「有狗」「有袋鼠」往往最容易

Tip: “has a dog” and “has kangaroos” are usually easiest to lock first

小明收到的是？

Mike’s card is?
