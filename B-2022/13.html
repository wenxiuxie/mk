阿拉丁的正方形地毯，每边两排圆点，点数相同。地毯一角折起来了。一共有多少个圆点？

Aladdin’s square carpet has two equal rows of dots along each side. A corner folded over. How many dots in total?

实验：掀开折角，并在空格点点来数

Lab: lift the fold; tap blank cells to place dots while counting
